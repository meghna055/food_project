When putting together food baskets for the needy, you need to keep in mind that everything should be nonperishable. The organization that will distribute the baskets likely won't accept fresh fruit, vegetables and baked goods. Make sure that nonperishables aren't expired or about to expire -- and that the packaging is sealed.
